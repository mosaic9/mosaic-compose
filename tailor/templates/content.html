Mosaic & docker-compose

Welcome to the mosaic demo website. While exploring this codebase you will get some idea of how different pieces of mosaic fit together, we have quite a few moving parts here. Try to understand how each one of them actually gets rendered.

Templates

This text is rendered from the content.html template in tailor/templates directory. That particular file is used, because skipper runs setPath("/content") in the tailor route (so there's a convention on how templates are picked-up based on path).

Slots

You can use slots to inject information in predefined places of a tailor template from a fragment or (in this case) a child template.
